About Us

ApplyTogether is a non-profit that helps high-achieving students earn scholarships to attend colleges with high graduation rates. We provide these high school students with the best standardized test prep available, and pair them with a mentor who understands the college application process.

Meet the team

Michael Booker

CEO

Michael Booker was the valedictorian of the class of 2010 at Greenville-Weston High School in Greenville, Mississippi. He is currently studying electrical and computer engineering at the University of Texas at Austin, where he is a Gateway Scholar. He is the first person in his family to attend college.

Patrick Brown

Founder

Patrick graduated from Georgetown University, where he was a voting member of the College Admissions Committee. After graduating, he taught high school social studies in the Mississippi Delta through Teach for America, and counseled students on college applications. He is currently a student at Harvard Law School.

Michelle Skinner

Secretary

Michelle graduated from Butler University, where she majored in English and Spanish. After graduating, she taught middle school reading and writing in Itta Bena, Mississippi, through Teach for America. Michelle is currently a teacher at Roxbury Preparatory Charter School in Massachussetts.

Paul Campbell

Treasurer

Paul graduated from Georgetown University, where he won the international business faculty award for academic excellence. He is currently a Senior Consultant at the strategy consulting firm Monitor Group, and an alumni interviewer for the Georgetown Alumni Admissions Program.

Samantha Hoare

Board Member

Samantha is a Managing Director of Teacher Leadership Development with Teach For America in the Mississippi Delta, and has spent two years developing a program to promote college success in Belize. Samantha holds a master's degree from the University of the West Indies and a bachelor's degree from Duke University.

John T. Tighe III

Secretary

John T. Tighe III is the Founder, President and CEO of TMG Health, Inc. He started the company in 1998, and it has grown from one employee to over 1,200 employees. He is a graduate of the University of Pittsburgh with a BS in Pharmacy and received a Masters in Business Administration from Villanova University.
